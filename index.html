Oren Sultan

Computer Science PhD Candidate

The Hebrew University

 

I am a Computer Science PhD candidate (direct track) in the Rachel and Selim Benin School of Computer Science & Engineering (CS department) at The Hebrew University 🇮🇱, grateful to be advised by Prof. Dafna Shahaf, also working part-time as an AI Researcher & Data Scientist at Lightricks

I completed both my MSc & BSc in Computer Science at Hebrew University & participated in two semester-long exchange programs with scholarships for honors students. In my BSc I joined The School of Computing and Information Systems at The University of Melbourne (UniMelb) 🇦🇺, there I worked with international teams on my first research projects in AI and ML. In my MSc I joined The School of Computation, Information and Technology at The Technical University of Munich (TUM) 🇩🇪, there I participated in NLP Lab course, worked with my team on a research project under Prof. Georg Groh.
